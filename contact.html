We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (1-2 business days), and same-day delivery in select metropolitan areas. Free standard shipping is provided for orders over $100. International shipping is available to select countries with delivery times varying based on location.

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for unworn, unwashed items with original tags and packaging. Returns are free for customers in the United States. For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs. Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after we receive your return.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and link. You can also track your order by logging into your BrightWeb account and visiting the "Order History" section. If you haven't received a tracking number within 48 hours of receiving your order confirmation, please contact our customer service team.
